East Asian Union
On the extremely diverse University of California, Berkeley campus, the East Asian Union functions as an inclusive platform uniting the East Asian community and bridging gaps within. Each academic semester, we host 1-2 very grand events every semester where all active clubs join together, pool their resources, and form committees to work together to celebrate the diversity that brings us together on one campus at Cal. We are an all volunteer organization dedicated to raising cultural awareness by collaborating with our partners.
EAU Night Market
Every semester, EAU hosts a night market where many cultural groups come together to offer a taste of their home country's traditional foods and to put together cultural performances.
Night Market is happening on November 8th this semester from 6:00 - 9:00 PM! More information can be found on our Facebook page.

Taiwanese American Student Association (TASA)
TASA is a social and cultural student organization celebrating everything Taiwanese American. We welcome people from all cultural backgrounds as long as you are curious, passionate, or have a heart for Taiwanese culture. We hold weekly events involving yummy food, fun activities, and amazing people. We are more than just a club -- we are family!

Korean Undergraduate Networking Association (KUNA)
The Korean Undergraduate Networking Association, better known as “KUNA”, is a fundamental centerpiece for the Korean Community at the University of California, Berkeley. Our mission is to provide Korean students, international and American alike, with networking opportunities that help mold the individual’s professional undergraduate career. Through a variety of events, including but not limited to ones centering around culture, community, and professionality, we seek to provide both social and professional opportunities to the Korean students on campus. We also participate in school-wide events to further promote and strengthen the exposure of Korean culture and tradition.
Asian American Association (AAA)
The purpose of the Asian American Association (AAA) is to be at the forefront of uniting the AsianAmerican Community at Berkeley. AAA seeks to develop proactive and community-oriented leaders through a social, educational awareness and service network. It enhances the quality of life at Berkeley through its focus on diversity appreciation.
Taiwanese Student Association (TSA)
The Taiwanese Student Association (TSA) at UC Berkeley is a non-profit, non-political student organization that focuses on enriching the college experience of anyone who shares common interest in Taiwanese culture. Our goal is mainly to represent the Taiwanese community and strengthen its voice on and beyond the campus.
